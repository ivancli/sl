  • Uses a high powered Bridgelux LED chip

  • 2-way tilt adjustable
  • Up to 70% energy saving
  • Full dimmable and C-BUS compatible with Universal and trailing edge dimmers
  • Cutout: 75mm
  • Colours: White, Black or Silver

  • Lumens: 720LM (3000K), 740LM (5600K)

  • Lifetime: 50000 Hours
  • CRI: > 90
